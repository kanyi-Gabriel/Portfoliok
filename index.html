My Story: From Engineering to Data

My journey into data began with a unique foundation: Electrical and Electronic Engineering. As a final-year student at the Technical University of Mombasa, I developed a deep understanding of complex systems, automation, and hardware. I’ve programmed Siemens PLCs to manage traffic flow and built Arduino-based IoT systems to regulate water supplies.

This engineering mindset—focused on efficiency, systems-thinking, and building things that work—is the same one I now apply to the world of data. I saw a powerful opportunity to merge physical automation with intelligent, data-driven decisions. To bridge this gap, I pursued formal Data Science certifications from ALX Africa and WorldQuant University, solidifying my expertise in turning raw data into actionable, automated solutions.
